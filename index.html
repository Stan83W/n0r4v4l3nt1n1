Nora Valentini is a multidisciplinary graphic designer with a focus on art direction, identity UI design and digital technologies. Graduated in 2016 from the CNAM, Paris (conservatoire national des arts et métiers) in graphic and UI design, she had since worked as Freelancer for clients in private sector in Europe and United-States.
In freelance since 2016, she’s based in Paris and she co-founded Valentini-Wiart studio in 2019. Working in the fields of design, fashion, architecture, music, tech and innovative industry.

Constantly abreast of new experiences, her services inclued art direction, brand identity, UI design and web integration. Her aim is to deliver meaningful digital products and digital experiences that blend design and technology. She’s allways looking for challenges that can make her imaginate, interprate, create and improve myself. Self initiated projects and personal research are an integral part of her work.
